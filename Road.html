Road
Giant Propel Advanced SL 0
A bicycle specialized for high-speed riding on paved roads.
It is also called ‘cycle’ or ‘road’.
In general, it is equipped with large, thin wheels and drop bars and has no suspension,
so it is the closest bicycle to its original form, except for fixed gear bikes,
which are direct descendants of bicycle bicycles.
If compared to a car, it is equivalent to a sports car.
