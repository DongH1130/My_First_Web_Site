MTB
2023 Giant Anthem Advance PRO 29 1
Abbreviation for Mountain Terrain Bike.
This refers to a bicycle for mountain terrain.
In contrast to road bikes, which are specialized for paved roads,
this bike is designed to be ridden on mountain roads filled with rocks, tree roots, gravel, stairs, and ledges.
It has a design that focuses on riding rough terrain, including a sturdy body,
a gear ratio that focuses on low speeds compared to road bikes,
and a shock absorbing structure with suspension at the front or front/rear of the bike.
